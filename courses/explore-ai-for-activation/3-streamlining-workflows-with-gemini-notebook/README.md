# Streamlining Workflows with NotebookLM

## What students learn
Students learn how to turn large, mixed-source information into useful outputs with NotebookLM. They practice source ingestion, grounded Q and A across selected sources, web source discovery, slide deck generation, and Studio features such as mind maps, flashcards, reports, and audio summaries.

## Case study
This course uses Cymbal Pharma, where teams must synthesize dense scientific and operational information from many sources. Learners apply NotebookLM to realistic research and communication tasks common in drug development and medical knowledge work. In the final lab, students step outside the scenario entirely — defining and building a notebook for an information challenge of their own choosing.
