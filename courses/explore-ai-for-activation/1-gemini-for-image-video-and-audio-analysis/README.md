# Gemini for Image, Video, and Audio Analysis

## What students learn
Students learn how to use Gemini and related Google AI tools to create and analyze visual and audio content for business outcomes. Across the plays, they practice image generation, infographic creation, meta prompting for stronger visual prompts, video compliance analysis, and short-form promotional video and audio production.

## Case study
This course uses Cymbal-Mart, a fictional omnichannel retailer with physical stores, warehouses, and e-commerce operations. Learners apply multimodal AI to realistic retail scenarios such as promotions, merchandising quality checks, and social marketing content. In the final lab, students step outside the scenario entirely — choosing a real use case from their own work and applying the course skills independently.

## Labs

| # | Lab | Description |
|---|-----|-------------|
| 1 | [Ice Breaker](1-ice-breaker/ice-breaker.md) | Use Gemini's image generation model to place yourself into a Cymbal-Mart warehouse setting using your own headshot. |

| 3 | [Meta Prompting for Image Generation](3-meta-prompting-for-image-generation/meta-prompting-for-image-generation.md) | Ask Gemini to write an image generation prompt for you, then use that AI-generated prompt to produce a luxury lifestyle marketing asset. |
| 4 | [Video Analysis](4-video-analysis/video-analysis.md) | Upload a store walk-through video alongside a standards PDF and ask Gemini to cross-reference them for compliance violations with timestamps and citations. |
| 5 | [Video and Audio Generation](5-video-and-audio-generation/video-and-audio-generation.md) | Generate a holiday social media video clip, write a synchronized voiceover script, and produce an audio track using Google AI Studio. |
| 6 | [Bring Your Own Use Case](6-bring-your-own-use-case/bring-your-own-use-case.md) | Apply the full multimodal workflow independently — choose image generation, infographic creation, video analysis, or video and audio production for a use case of your own design. |
